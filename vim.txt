Left shft + 5    -> to jump to corresponding closing braces

To insert common word in selected line
Shift+v, shift+ i  , add required word/character, ESC

To delete starting word/alpha of sequential lines
    Place cursor on first or last word to del
    Press Ctrl+v to enter Visual Block mode
    Use arrow keys to select the ; characters you want to delete (or the other "first few characters")
    Press x to delete them all at once
